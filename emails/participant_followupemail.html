Participate in a Research Study!

Details about the the survey:

Your responses will help us to understand what we should do more and less of at hackathons so you (and future hackers) can thrive at student hackathons. Will you please help us with our work and complete this 3-5 minute survey?

The survey is completely anonymous. Your personal information is not requested and the database does not store it. You can quit the survey at any time. You must be 18 or older to complete this survey. This survey is being administered by PhD candidate, Brittany Kos, at the University of Colorado Boulder. If you have questions about the study, please contact her at brittany.kos@colorado.edu. (And we're serious about it being 3-5 minutes, it's about 30 questions, most of them agree-disagree!)

If you wish to participate in the survey, please follow this link: https://cuboulder.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_3mHEjRYVpieJDxj.
