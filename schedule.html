Detailed Schedule

Note: This schedule is tentative and subject to change, times are approximate. Descriptions and workshops will be added in the future!

Sat, Feb 10

  • 10:00a
    1st Floor Lobby
    Check-In
    The hackathon is open! Come in and check in.
  • 10:30a
    ATLAS 100
    Opening Ceremonies
    Welcome to the hackathon! We'll talk about the hackathon logistics and hear from our sponsors and partners. We'll also go over the events and challenges that will be happening.
  • 11:00a
    1st Floor Lobby
    Lunch, Ice Breaker, Team Formation
    Meet with other T9Hackers. Meet team members, get feedback on project ideas. Come meet our sponsors!
  • 12:00p
    1st, 2nd, and 1B Floor Rooms
    Hacking Begins
    Let's go!
  • 12:00p
    1st Floor Rooms
    Workshops Begin
  • 6:00p
    1st Floor Lobby
    Dinner

Sun, Feb 11

  • 8:00a
    1st Floor Lobby
    Breakfast
  • 12:00p
    All Teams
    Hacking Ends
    You can stop making now! Please submit all projects to Devpost and prepare for demos. If you've never used Devpost before, you can find information about submitting projects through the website here: T9Hacks Live Page
  • 12:00p
    1st Floor Lobby
    Lunch
  • 12:30p
    ATLAS 100
    Project Demos
    Time to show-off what you've created! Demos will be lecture-style, so everyone can get a chance to see other T9Hacker projects. We encourage everyone to demo, even if you didn't get your whole project done. Show off what you accomplished, someone else could learn from your successes too!
  • 3:00p
    ATLAS 100
    Closing Ceremonies
    A final thank you to everyone who made this possible! We'll also be giving out awards for the hacking challenges.
  • 3:30p
    All Teams
    Hackathon Ends!
    Sleepy time!

Feb 10-11, 2018
University of Colorado Boulder
